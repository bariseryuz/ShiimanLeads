# Shiiman Leads – Support & SLA

## Support channels

- **Email:** Use the contact address shown in the app (e.g. guidebaris@outlook.com) or your account’s support contact.
- **In-app:** Use the notifications and dashboard for status; contact support for account or billing issues.

## Response times

- **Standard (included):** We aim to respond to support requests within **1 business day**.
- **Urgent (e.g. service down):** We aim to acknowledge within **4 hours** during business hours.

Business hours are defined as 9:00–17:00 in the service operator’s timezone unless stated otherwise in your agreement.

## Uptime

- **Target uptime:** 99% measured monthly (excluding planned maintenance).
- **Planned maintenance:** We will announce scheduled maintenance in advance when possible. Maintenance windows do not count against uptime.
- **Incidents:** We will post status updates and work to restore service as quickly as possible.

## What “uptime” means

Uptime is measured as the percentage of time the main application (login, dashboard, API, and scrape runs) is available. Excluded:

- Third-party source sites being down or blocking access
- Your network or device issues
- Actions you take (e.g. pausing sources, account suspension for non-payment)

## Data and backups

- We run automated database backups. See **RESTORE.md** for restore instructions.
- You are responsible for exporting important data if you need your own copies.

## Billing and subscription

- Billing is handled by Paddle. Failed payments are subject to a grace period (see app or billing emails). After that, access may be restricted until payment is updated.
- Refund and cancellation terms are in the [Terms of Service](/terms.html).
